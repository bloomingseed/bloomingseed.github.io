Research Projects

Master's Thesis Research

Title: Research on RGB-D based Multi-task Deep Learning Framework for Outdoor Navigation Assistants for Visual Impairment (ONAVIs)
Supervisor: Assoc. Prof. 高广宇

My proposed Master's thesis sets the focus on applying latest advancements in Computer Vision, Deep Neural Network to solve the problem of outdoor navigation of people with blind and visual impairment in urban environment. The core AI model will fuse 2 modalities - camera image and depth (or disparity) - and performs object detection + semantic segmentation multitask output.

Building upon this core model is a full pipeline for processing its output to make reasoning, summarization and produce AI synthesized human voice to notify end-user about the environment - obstacles, walkable space and their distance, angle to camera view.

The dataset used for the model is synthesized using CARLA simulator.

October 2025 - Now
Location: Beijing Institute of Technology, Haidian district, Beijing, China
Skills: LaTeX, Academic research, Literature review, Research papers, Computer vision, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, CARLA simulator
